Hi — I'm Ariama Victor (ovac4u). I'm the Founder & CEO of Gidipex, building at the intersection of core AI programming, applied business modelling & strategy, and crypto technologies. Gidipex is where I turn research-grade ideas into shippable products — AI systems that actually do work in the real world, with the business model and on-chain infrastructure to match.

I'm also a co-founder of Bitnormous, and recently served as Tech & Team Lead at Payplux Ghana — leading engineering and shipping payments infrastructure into production. Earlier I founded RescopeNet, a studio that contracts engineering work and ships its own products.

On the engineering side I work primarily in PHP/Laravel, TypeScript/Node, React/Next.js and Python for AI work. A lot of my open-source time goes into Laravel tooling and LLM/MCP integrations: idoc for API documentation (185+ ★), laravel-mcp-inspector for inspecting MCP servers from Laravel, and guardrails — an operational approvals engine that routes every high-risk Laravel change through multi-step reviewer sign-off.

I take on a limited number of advisory engagements each year — usually for founders working on AI-native or crypto-native products. Outside code: long-form reading, travel, and the occasional side project that escapes containment.
